Partnerships keep the foundation steady for students and families. The organization welcomes thoughtful collaboration that aligns with its grounded model.

## Ways to help
- Fund daily nutrition so students receive 2-3 meals each day
- Underwrite healthcare access and family advocacy that keep learners present
- Back the capital campaign to eliminate rental dependency
- Partner with governance teams for transparent financial review

## What partners can expect
- Calm, factual reporting on how resources are applied
- Access to program and governance documentation for compliance reviews
- Long-term alignment to the Whole Child / Obstacle Removal model
