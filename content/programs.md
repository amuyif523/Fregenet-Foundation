Each campus is rooted in the same whole-child design while responding to local contexts in Addis Ababa and Bishoftu.

## Fregenet Kidan Lehitsanat - Addis Ababa (Kirkos Sub-City)
- Serves students in the heart of Addis Ababa with nutrition, healthcare access, and family advocacy alongside academics
- Partnerships with nearby services keep healthcare and documentation support close to the classroom

## Fregenet Dembi Elementary - Bishoftu
- Provides a stable learning environment for families in Bishoftu with the same 1:30 classroom attention standard
- Community engagement keeps families informed on student progress and available supports

## Alumni support through university
- Graduates remain connected for guidance as they move through secondary school and university
- Alumni receive mentoring to stay focused on studies while navigating emerging responsibilities
