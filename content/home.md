Fregenet Foundation is a long-standing Ethiopian education nonprofit founded in 2004 to honor the legacy of Fregenet Leeza Tafesse Woubshet. The foundation serves children in Addis Ababa and Bishoftu through a grounded, whole-child model that removes barriers to learning and builds lasting trust with families and partners.

## A grounded education model
- Whole Child / Obstacle Removal approach centered on learning readiness
- Nutrition: 2-3 meals each day based on student need
- Healthcare access coordinated with local providers
- Family advocacy that stabilizes home environments
- Classroom ratio of 1:30 to maintain attention and care

## Programs with depth
- Fregenet Kidan Lehitsanat - Addis Ababa (Kirkos Sub-City)
- Fregenet Dembi Elementary - Bishoftu
- Alumni support through university to sustain academic momentum

## Capital campaign for permanence
- 1,670 m2 of land secured from the Addis Ababa City Administration
- Permanent multi-story facility designed by Blu Dot Architects & Engineers
- Goal: remove rental dependency and safeguard continuity for students

## Governance that earns trust
- President self-funds administrative travel
- Registered Ethiopian Foreign Charity and US 501(c)(3)
- Transparent financial reporting for institutional partners
- Active collaborations with banks, embassies, and international NGOs
- Governance practices documented and reviewed annually
