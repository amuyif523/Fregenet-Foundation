The foundation welcomes conversations with donors, public agencies, and partners who align with the whole-child model. Use the inquiry form to request documentation, discuss the capital campaign, or coordinate program support.

Please share enough detail to route your request to the right team member. The foundation keeps correspondence clear, factual, and focused on student readiness.

Information you submit is used only to respond to your inquiry and is not shared beyond the foundation and its designated administrators.
